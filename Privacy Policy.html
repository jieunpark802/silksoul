Privacy Policy

At Silksoul, we prioritize your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you engage with our platform.

1. Data Collection

We collect minimal personal information, such as email addresses, solely for the purpose of facilitating transactions, providing updates, and enhancing your experience. Your data is never shared or sold to third parties.

2. Use of Information

Any data collected is used to provide you with seamless access to our NFTs, notify you of updates, and ensure the security of your transactions. We use state-of-the-art encryption to protect your information at every step.

3. Cookies

Silksoul uses cookies to improve your browsing experience. These cookies are anonymous and do not store personal information. You can disable cookies in your browser settings if preferred.

4. Blockchain Transparency

Due to the decentralized nature of blockchain technology, certain transactions may be publicly visible on the blockchain. However, personal details are not linked to blockchain transactions within our platform.

5. Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect improvements to our platform or changes in regulations. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically.

If you have any concerns or requests regarding your personal data, please contact us.
